FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is GamerStop?

GamerStop is an online platform that offers exclusive gaming merchandise and a collection of free-to-play games for gamers worldwide.

What can I find in the GamerStop store?

Our store features exclusive gaming-related tech gadgets, apparel, and accessories inspired by popular games.

What types of free games are available?

We offer a curated selection of free-to-play games across different genres. These games can be accessed and played directly from our platform.

How do I purchase items from the store?

Simply browse the store, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept multiple payment options for a secure shopping experience.

Are the free games really free to play?

Yes! All games in our "Games" section are free-to-play. Some may have optional in-game purchases, but you can play the base game at no cost.

Do I need an account to use GamerStop?

Yes, an account is required to purchase merchandise, access free games, and stay updated on new releases.

How can I track my orders?

Once your order is placed, you will receive an email with tracking details. You can also check your order status in your GamerStop account.

What is GamerStop’s refund policy?

We accept returns for unused physical merchandise within 30 days. Digital products and free games are non-refundable.

How do I get support if I have an issue?

If you need assistance, you can send us a message through your GamerStop account or check our FAQ page for common questions.

Is GamerStop available worldwide?

Yes! However, shipping for merchandise may be limited to certain regions, and some free games may have regional restrictions.

How often are new free games added?

We frequently update our game collection with new free-to-play titles. Check the "Games" section regularly for new additions.

Do I need to download games to play them?

Some games may require a download, while others can be played directly in your browser. Check the game’s description for details.

Can I save my progress in free games?

Yes, most games allow you to save progress, either through cloud saves or locally on your device. Some games may require an account for saving.

Are there age restrictions for certain games?

Yes, some games have age restrictions based on content. You must be of legal age in your country to access these games.

Can I play GamerStop free games on any device?

Most games are available on PC, but some may work on mobile or console. Check each game’s requirements before playing.
